The Protest

threatening biodiversity and water security. The protests prompted the Supreme Court to pause its own ruling and announce a review.

Government Response

In response to the protests and legal challenges, the Indian government has indicated a willingness to revisit the definition of the Aravalli Hills.
Officials have acknowledged the ecological importance of the entire range and expressed commitment to balancing development needs with environmental conservation.
A committee comprising environmental experts, geologists, and local stakeholders has been proposed to study the issue and recommend a more inclusive definition that protects critical areas while allowing for sustainable development.
The government has also emphasized stricter enforcement of existing environmental regulations to curb illegal mining and deforestation in the Aravalli region.
